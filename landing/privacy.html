1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app usage to improve the Service
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by our payment provider; we do not store payment details

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we never access:

  • Your Instagram password (authentication happens on your device)
  • Your direct messages content
  • Your Instagram followers or following lists
  • Photos or media from your conversations

3. Local Data Storage

Raiden stores data locally on your device including:

  • Conversation history for AI context (never uploaded)
  • AI-generated persona profiles
  • App settings and preferences

This data remains on your computer and you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.

4. AI Processing

When generating AI responses:

  • Message context is sent to our AI service for processing
  • Messages are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers
  • AI-generated responses are returned to your device and not logged

5. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important updates about the Service
  • Improve the Service based on usage patterns

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With service providers who assist in operating the Service
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a merger or acquisition (with prior notice)

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Regular security audits

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Export your data in a portable format

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the Service.
